# Story 4.4: Global Filters & Period Selector

---

## Status

**In Progress**

---

## Story Statement

As a member of the internal team, I want global filters for period, source, medium, and campaign, so that I can analyze data for specific time ranges and traffic segments.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Barra de filtros persistente no topo do dashboard: Period Selector, Source filter, Medium filter, Campaign filter
2. Period Selector com presets: Hoje, Ultimos 7 dias, Ultimos 30 dias (padrao), Ultimos 90 dias, Custom
3. Filtros Source, Medium e Campaign sao dropdowns populados dinamicamente
4. Filtros sao compostos — selecionar Source + Campaign mostra intersecao
5. Todos os componentes reagem aos filtros em tempo real sem reload
6. Estado dos filtros persiste na URL via query params para compartilhamento
7. Botao "Limpar Filtros" reseta todos ao padrao
