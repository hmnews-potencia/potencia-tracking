# Story 3.1: Tracking Script (Vanilla JS)

---

## Status

**Done**

---

## Story Statement

As a member of the internal team, I want a lightweight tracking script that can be embedded in any website, so that I can capture pageviews, UTM attribution, and conversions from external sites.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Script em vanilla JS (`/public/tracking/pt-tracker.js`) sem dependencias externas
2. Le UTM params da URL e persiste em localStorage
3. Gera/recupera `tracking_id` (UUID v4) em cookie `_ptk_id` com expiracao de 180 dias
4. Envia evento `pageview` para `/api/track` com: tracking_id, timestamp, UTM params, page_url, referrer, user_agent
5. Expoe metodo global `PotenciaTracker.trackConversion(data)` para eventos de conversao
6. Configuracao via atributo data no script tag: `<script src="...pt-tracker.js" data-project="slug" data-api="https://...">`
7. Deduplicacao: nao envia pageview duplicado na mesma sessao (sessionStorage flag)
8. Nao impacta LCP da pagina hospedeira — carregamento async/defer obrigatorio
